Ready to get started?

These steps are strongly recomended for (Exppert) DIY users who decided to go for DIY projects

1

Website Builder

First of all you need to visit our website builder by login at VistaPanel, choose your design by clicking on selected template or by clicking on continue button.

2

Preview

After that click on your preffered choice of design and preview it, if you want to continue with the selected design, click on continue button to proceed further.

3

Authorise

Fill up your ftp account details like username and password and ftp server address, this selected design will autometically upload to your ftp server.

4

Download

Login to our VistaPanel using your username and password, download your all files from the "htdocs" folder using our online ftp manager or our recomended ftp software.

5

Customise

Open your all files using any web editor or text editor application, get customised your design as per your requirement by editing your .css, .js, .jquerry and .html files.

6

Finish

Finish your work by uploading your all updated files to your ftp server, if system ask for overwrite the files kindly click on yes to upload new files in the place of old files.
